About me

Ana Paula Armendáriz Salas

I am Dr. Ana Paula Armendáriz, a dentist passionate about oral health care and transforming smiles. My academic training began at the Central University of Ecuador, where I earned my dental degree, and later I completed a Master's in Orthodontics and Maxillofacial Orthopedics at the Autonomous University of Nuevo León, Mexico. This allowed me to specialize in high-quality treatments for dental health and aesthetics.

At Orthosadent, my mission is to provide comprehensive and personalized care, based on science, technology, and a close human approach. I am convinced that every smile is unique, so I design customized treatment plans for each patient, always prioritizing health, comfort, and confidence.

My values are ethics, responsibility, empathy, and professional excellence. I firmly believe that dentistry not only improves smiles but also people’s lives, and that is why I commit every day to providing quality service with warmth and dedication.
